Attack on Titan: an anime series that has gained a massive following around the world.
The show takes place in a dystopian world where humans are forced to live within walls to protect themselves from Titans,
giant humanoid creatures who devour humans without reason. The story is full of intense action scenes,
heart-wrenching moments, and unexpected plot twists. Moreover, the show's animation and soundtrack are stunning;
the use of color, light, and shadow is particularly noteworthy.
Overall, Attack on Titan is a must-watch for anyone who enjoys dark, thrilling anime series.
